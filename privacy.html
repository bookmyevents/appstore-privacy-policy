EventAura Privacy

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how EventAura collects, uses, and shares information when you use the EventAura iOS application.

Effective date: March 28, 2026

Information we collect

  • Phone number and verification data to sign you in with one-time password authentication.
  • Vendor registration details if you choose to create a vendor listing.
  • Event planning inputs such as event type, location, guest count, budget, date, and preferences.
  • On-device preference data such as login state, saved phone number, vendor status, and favorites.
  • Technical verification data used by Firebase Authentication for phone sign-in and anti-abuse checks.

How we use information

  • To authenticate users and maintain sign-in state.
  • To create and display vendor listings inside the app.
  • To provide vendor discovery, package recommendations, and event planning assistance.
  • To support basic app functionality such as favorites and profile display.
  • To protect the service against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.

How information is shared

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the app. EventAura currently uses Firebase Authentication from Google to support phone number sign-in and verification flows. Information submitted as a vendor may also be displayed to other users inside the app as part of your listing.

Data storage

Some information is stored locally on your device, including login state, your phone number, vendor status, and favorites. Authentication and verification-related information may be processed by Firebase Authentication according to Google’s privacy practices.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the app’s functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Locally stored data remains on your device until you remove the app or clear that data.

Your choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can sign out from within the app.
  • You can choose whether to submit vendor information or event planning details.
  • You can remove locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device.

Children’s privacy

EventAura is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Third-party services

Third-party services may have their own privacy policies. For Firebase Authentication, see Firebase Privacy and Security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date shown on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, please use the developer contact information provided in the EventAura App Store listing or support channel.

This policy reflects the current behavior of the EventAura iOS app as of March 28, 2026.
